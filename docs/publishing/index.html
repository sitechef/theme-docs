Publish Your Theme

Before publishing your theme, ensure that all pages load correctly without javascript or css errors.

We highly recommend trying your theme on a number of browsers first.

Ensure that you run sitechef serve at least once before the final publish to check that everything works.

Once ready to publish execute sitechef publish.

Your theme will now be published back to SiteChef and be available on your or your client's website.
